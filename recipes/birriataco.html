Birria Taco

A classic Birria Taco recipe from the streets of Mexico with a great spice and juicy, cheesy combo!

  • Tortilla
  • Hot Peppers
  • Beef Chuck
  • Oaxaca Cheese
  • Stock, Cilantro, Onions

Steps

  1. Fry onions with tomato paste and the peppers, and stock
  2. Blend, then add your seared beef chuck
  3. Cook for long hours, then shred meat
  4. Toast tortilla with consome, the meat, and cheese, and serve with more consome!
