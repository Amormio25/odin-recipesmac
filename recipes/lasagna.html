Lasagna

This world-class phenomenon Garfield Lasagna is an easy 5 ingredient recipe, requiring the likes of luscious tomatoes, ground beef, cheese, spinach, and pasta. Simply cook a tomato, ground beef sauce and layer with pasta and cheese for the ultimate dish!

  • pasta
  • tomatoes/tomato sauce
  • ground beef
  • cheese
  • spinach

Steps

  1. Add the tomatoes and thr ground beef to cook on a pot
  2. Cook spinach and mold into pasta shapes
  3. Layer with the ground beef sauce and cheese
  4. Bake
