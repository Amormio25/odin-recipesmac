Pho

Soulful, warming, comforting Vietnamese Beef Pho! Arguably the greatest soup of all time!

  • Spices
  • Beef of Choice
  • Rice Noodles
  • Cilantro, Onions, Lime
  • Beef Stock

Steps

  1. Boil the cleaned, boiled beef with all the grilled aromatics, onions, fish sauce, and beef stock
  2. Cook rice noodles
  3. Prepare bowl with the sliced meat and sides of bean sprouts, hoisin sauce, and lime
  4. Add broth to the bowl and noodles and serve!
